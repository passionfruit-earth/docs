#### Overview
Your personal settings and preferences in Passionfruit.

<Info> Managed in the app only: Profile can only be managed from the Passionfruit app. See the [App & Integrations](/docs/installation/apps)  for where to find it.</Info>


Open Profile by clicking your avatar in the top left, then Settings, then Profile.
<img src="/images/workspace/profile.png" className="w-full border-none h-auto my-0" alt="Profile settings in Passionfruit" />


You can update your display name, job title, and contact information here, and you can change your password and manage sign-in preferences (contact your IT manager).
