#### Overview

We **search** your knowledge using your question, **entities** (which company or subsidiary), and your [search configuration](/docs/fill-questionnaires/search-configuration). Then we check whether you already have a **reusable answer**.

The **Answer Library** holds **approved answers** your team saved from past questionnaires: answers you generated before and chose to keep. When search finds a strong match, we can return that reusable answer.

If there isn’t one, we **generate a new answer** by drawing on what’s in your workspace: information from **answers** (including what’s tied to entities) and from **Evidence** (your documents and files). The new answer is grounded in that material and includes citations you can verify.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search with your context">
    We run a search over your knowledge using the question, **entities**,
    questionnaire, framework, and tags.     Your [search
    configuration](/docs/fill-questionnaires/search-configuration) limits which evidence and
    Answer Library entries we consider.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Look for a reusable Answer Library answer">
    We check whether an **approved, reusable answer** already exists in your
    **Answer Library** (something your team saved from an earlier
    questionnaire). If search finds a good match, we surface that answer
    (including colleague context where it helps).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Otherwise generate a new answer">
    If there isn’t a reusable library answer, we **generate a new one**: we
    search **approved answer content and entity-related information** already in
    your workspace, together with your **Evidence**, and draft a response from
    that material. We add **citations** and you can use **Review** to inspect
    sources.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sources

1. **Answer Library:** Approved answers you saved from past questionnaires; we return one when search finds a **reusable** match.
2. **Answer content and entities:** When we **generate** a new answer, we also draw on stored answer text and entity-linked detail (not only files).
3. **Evidence:** Documents and files we search and cite when building or supporting an answer.
4. **Scope:** Entities and search configuration limit what we search so answers stay tied to the right company and sources.

## Citations

- Every suggested answer includes citations and deep links
- Open sources to check details before using the answer

## Limits

- May ask for clarification if context is missing
- Can be uncertain about very new or unapproved content
