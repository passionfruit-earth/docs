---
title: 'Delete entities'
description: 'Remove entities that are outdated or incorrect'
---

<Tabs>
    <Tab title="App" icon="browser">
    1. Click `Entities`
    2. Select one or more entities
    3. Click `Delete` and confirm
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Extension" icon="mobile">
    1. Go to `Home` in the extension
    2. Click `Entities`
    3. Select one or more entities
    4. Click `Delete` and confirm
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Notes:
- Deleting removes the entity and its relationships; attached evidence remains available
- Admins can restore within your retention window (if enabled)
