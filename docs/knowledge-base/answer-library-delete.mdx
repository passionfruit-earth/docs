#### Overview
Delete answers that are outdated or incorrect. This removes them from reuse; evidence remains linked.

<Tabs>
    <Tab title="App" icon="browser">
    1. Click `Answer Library`
    2. Select one or more answers
    3. Click `Delete` and confirm
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Extension" icon="mobile">
    - You can view and insert approved answers
    - Deletion happens in the App
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Are citations to Evidence deleted?">
    No. Deleting an answer removes it from reuse, but citations to Evidence are not deleted.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can admins restore deleted answers?">
    Yes. Admins can restore deleted answers within your retention window (if enabled).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
