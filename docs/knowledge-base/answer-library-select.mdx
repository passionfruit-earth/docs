### Overview
Answer Library lets you quickly find and reuse approved answers across questionnaires and documents.
Search by text and refine with filters (tags, entities, owners, approval date, frameworks), then insert into `Answers`.

### App
- **Search**: Search by question text and answer content.
- **Filters**: Filter by tags, entities, owners, approval date, and frameworks.

### Extension
- **Search**: Search and insert approved answers into your workflows.
- **Note**: Advanced filtering is available in the App.
