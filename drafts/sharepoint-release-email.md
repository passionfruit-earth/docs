# Email body

Hi {first_name},

Passionfruit is now connected to where your company data already lives.

With our new SharePoint integration, your policies, reports, and other documents are automatically pulled into Passionfruit. No more downloading files, re-uploading them, and hoping they're the latest version. Connect once, and your Knowledge Base stays in sync.

## Integrate with SharePoint

Connect SharePoint to Passionfruit and your documents flow into your Knowledge Base automatically. Your team works from the files you already maintain, without any manual steps in between.

→ [Read more about the SharePoint integration](https://docs.passionfruit.earth/docs/sharepoint)

## Keep a history of document changes

When a document is updated, Passionfruit saves it as a new evidence version instead of overwriting the previous one. You get a clear history of what changed and when, whether the update came from an integration or a manual upload.

→ [Read more about evidence versioning](https://docs.passionfruit.earth/docs/knowledge-base/evidence-versions)

## Reviewing approved answers

When source evidence changes, approved answers are automatically moved to **To review**. No one has to remember to check. Passionfruit surfaces what needs attention so your team can stay on top of it.

→ [Read more about evidence versioning](https://docs.passionfruit.earth/docs/knowledge-base/evidence-versions)

## Get started

Enable the SharePoint integration in your workspace and connect it to the right source. Once it's set up, keeping evidence up to date takes seconds instead of hours.

→ [Set up SharePoint](https://docs.passionfruit.earth/docs/sharepoint)
